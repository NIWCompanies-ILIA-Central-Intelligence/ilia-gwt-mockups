ⓘ What your client may need

Have these ready to complete enrollment — depending on the product and answers.
▸A valid, non-expired driver's license (front and back) or passport photo + signature page.
▸The two most recent years of W-2s or 1099s, or two years of tax returns.
▸If the license address differs from the current address, a recent utility bill (under 60 days).
▸A list of assets, liabilities, and beneficiary details (name, SSN, address, phone, email).
▸Any in-force life insurance policies — carrier, active date, and death benefit amounts.
