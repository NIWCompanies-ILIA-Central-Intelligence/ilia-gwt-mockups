What this demonstrates (for dev)

  • On Yes, the giftor's Kai-Zen policy fields (tranche, policy #, carrier, death benefit) are pulled from ILIA and auto-filled — no manual typing.
  • Auto-filled fields are read-only (greyed, not editable).
  • If the giftor has more than one policy, a dropdown appears to pick which one; selecting it repopulates the fields. (Try the "2 policies" toggle.)
  • If no policy is on file, no policy fields are shown (fallback). (Try "no policy on file.")
  • On No, the policy section stays hidden.
