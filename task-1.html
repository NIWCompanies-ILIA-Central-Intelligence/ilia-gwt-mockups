What this demonstrates (for dev)

  • Tab 1 — Giftor has a Kai-Zen policy: clicking Yes pulls the policy from ILIA and auto-fills read-only fields (tranche, policy #, carrier, death benefit). If more than one policy exists, a dropdown lets them pick which one.
  • Tab 2 — Giftor has no policy: clicking No shows the required fields the giftor must complete manually (editable, marked *).
  • Auto-filled fields are never editable; required (no-policy) fields are editable and validated.
